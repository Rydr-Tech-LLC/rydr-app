SafeRydr

Safety support designed around real ride concerns.

SafeRydr is the safety-focused layer in the Rydr app. It supports ride monitoring, safety reports, and optional recording workflows as they become available, with clear privacy expectations and restricted review access.

Ride monitoring

Trip status, driver details, route context, and shareable ETA keep trusted people informed.

Optional recording

SafeRydr recordings are intended for safety review, support, legal, or compliance needs.

Family-focused rides

Built for parents, family members, and elder transportation where clarity matters before pickup.

Support after the trip

Report incidents, dispute ride issues, request callbacks, and contact support from the app.

RydrBank

Rewards that turn completed rides into future value.

RydrBank tracks eligible completed rides. After every 10 completed rides of 5 miles or more in a matching ride group, riders can earn a banked ride code covering one trip up to 15 miles.

For drivers

More control over the work behind every ride.

Rydr is built around driver visibility: riders compare driver cards, driver-set rates are capped by tier for transparency, and fare breakdowns separate rider total, booking fee, platform share, and driver payout.

01Flexible availability

Drivers can appear to nearby riders when they are ready for the selected tier.

02Clear earning model

The app uses driver rates, minimums, booking fees, and a 60% ride subtotal payout model.

03Rider fit

Ratings, vehicle info, compliments, and ride tier help riders choose intentionally.

Community and events

Cash Rydr Hub gives riders and drivers a community-style request space.

Cash Rydr Hub is separate from standard Rydr booking. Riders can post scheduled ride requests, drivers can respond with offers, and users can manage messages, favorite drivers, visibility, and issue reporting.

Post ride requests

Pickup, destination, schedule, passengers, notes, budget, and ride type.

Choose visibility

Share with favorite drivers or the public Cash Hub community.

Manage offers

Compare driver responses, vehicle info, availability, and verification signals.
