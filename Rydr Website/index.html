RydrBank

Riders should feel rewarded for riding.

RydrBank tracks eligible ride progress toward future ride value. Riders can follow reward status, apply ride credits when eligible, and unlock free ride opportunities through consistent platform use.

Cash Rydr Hub supports the Rydr ecosystem as a community marketplace. It is separate from standard Rydr dispatched rides and gives riders and independent drivers a place to connect around local opportunities.
