Privacy & Third-Party Data Sharing

This page explains the categories of information Rydr Tech LLC collects, how that information is used, and when it may be shared with service providers that help operate the Rydr platform, website, mobile apps, and beta program.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Who This Applies To

This policy applies to riders, drivers, driver applicants, beta testers, website visitors, and others who interact with Rydr services.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, phone number, email address, preferred name, and login credentials.
  • Profile and onboarding details such as home address, driver license details, vehicle information, insurance or registration documents, uploaded photos, identity verification status, and background-check status where applicable.
  • Ride and marketplace information such as pickup and drop-off details, ride requests, trip status, selected ride type, driver availability, fares, fees, ratings, and support interactions.
  • Location information used to support ride requests, nearby driver matching, navigation, trip progress, safety, fraud prevention, and service improvements.
  • Payment and payout-related information processed through payment providers. Rydr does not intend to store full payment card numbers on its own servers.
  • Device, diagnostic, analytics, crash, performance, notification, and usage information used to operate, secure, and improve the platform.
  • Communications, feedback, beta reports, waitlist submissions, support requests, and operational messages.

How We Use Information

  • Provide rider, driver, beta, and support services.
  • Verify identity, authenticate accounts, and protect account security.
  • Process ride payments, authorization holds, refunds, payouts, and payment-related support.
  • Support driver onboarding, background screening, vehicle eligibility, safety review, and platform readiness.
  • Match ride requests, show trip progress, support navigation, and improve marketplace functionality.
  • Detect fraud, investigate safety incidents, enforce platform standards, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Send transactional messages about rides, payments, safety, support, account security, beta participation, and product updates.

Third-Party Data Sharing

Rydr may share information with service providers and technology partners when they help us deliver, secure, measure, or improve the platform. These providers may process information under their own terms and privacy practices as well as agreements with Rydr.

Location Information

Certain Rydr features require access to precise location while using the app. Location information may be used for ride requests, matching, navigation, trip progress, safety, fraud prevention, and service improvements.

Data Retention and Security

Rydr retains information for operational, legal, accounting, fraud prevention, safety, beta testing, and support purposes. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect personal information, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

Users may update account information, manage certain notification preferences, request account closure, or exercise privacy rights available under applicable law. Some information may need to be retained when required for legal, safety, accounting, fraud prevention, or dispute-resolution purposes.

Children's Privacy

Rydr services are not intended for individuals who are not legally eligible to use the platform under applicable law.

Updates and Contact

Rydr may update this policy periodically. Material changes may be communicated through the app, website, email, or other reasonable means.

Privacy questions can be sent to support@rydr-go.com.
