Transparent roadmap

CashRydr Hub is a planned beta expansion, not a finished promise.

The first priority is a controlled ride beta. CashRydr Hub gives Rydr a public direction for future local earning, partner campaigns, community tasks, and reward programs.

Phase 0

Invite-controlled ride beta

Start with a small Atlanta cohort, safety reporting, Mission Control approvals, and stable payments.

Phase 1

Rewards and partner pilots

Introduce simple campaigns, reward progress, referrals, and local partner visibility.

Phase 2

CashRydr earning hub

Expand into local opportunities, community activations, and measurable reports for growth funding.
